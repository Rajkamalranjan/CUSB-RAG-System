## 🎓 2. प्रवेश प्रक्रिया (Admission Process)

### Mode of Admission
Online

### Entrance Exam
CUET (Common University Entrance Test) for UG & PG courses

### Admission Steps
1. Apply for CUET entrance examination.
2. Appear in the CUET exam.
3. University prepares merit list based on CUET score and cut-off.
4. Selected candidates receive admission offer through registered email.
5. Candidates must pay admission fee within given deadline.
6. After payment, provisional admission is granted.
7. Students must complete document verification at the university.
8. If documents are valid, admission is confirmed.
9. If documents are incorrect, admission may be cancelled.

### CUET kya hai?
CUET ek national level entrance exam hai. *CUET is a national-level entrance exam.*
