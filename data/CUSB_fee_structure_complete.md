## ❓ Fee-Related QA Responses

### Q: M.Sc Statistics ki fees kitni hai?
**A:** M.Sc Statistics ki total fees **₹ 26,072** hai (2 saal ki). Yeh sabse affordable M.Sc programme hai CUSB mein.

Per semester breakdown:
- Tuition: ₹ 6,500
- Other fees: ₹ 1,000
- Total per semester: ₹ 7,500 approx

### Q: Sabse expensive course kaunsa hai CUSB mein?
**A:** CUSB mein sabse expensive course **M.Sc Biotechnology** hai jiski fees **₹ 94,072** hai (2 saal). Isme lab charges zyada hain.

### Q: Hostel ki fees kitni hai?
**A:** Hostel fees:
- Hostel Fee: ₹ 9,000 per semester
- Mess: ₹ 3,000 per month (₹ 36,000 per year)
- Total hostel + mess: ₹ 54,000 per year

### Q: PhD ki fees kitni hai?
**A:** PhD ki fees approximately **₹ 7,600 per semester** hai including:
- Tuition: ₹ 5,000
- Exam: ₹ 1,000
- Library: ₹ 500
- Sports: ₹ 500

### Q: B.Sc Agriculture ki fees kitni hai?
**A:** B.Sc (Hons.) Agriculture ki total fees approximately **₹ 2,00,000** hai (4 years).

### Q: Fees kab tak pay karni hai?
**A:** Admission offer ke baad fees pay karni hoti hai within given deadline. Semester fees start hone se pehle pay karni hoti hai.
