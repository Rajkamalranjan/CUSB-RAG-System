## Admission Process

### Eligibility for M.Sc Programmes:
- **Educational Qualification:** Bachelor's degree in relevant subject with minimum 50% marks (45% for SC/ST)
- **Entrance Exam:** CUET (Common University Entrance Test) PG
- **Mode:** Online application through CUSB Samarth Portal

### Mode of Admission:
1. Apply for CUET entrance examination
2. Appear in the CUET exam
3. University prepares merit list based on CUET score
4. Selected candidates receive admission offer
5. Pay admission fee within deadline
6. Complete document verification

### Important Dates (Typical):
- **CUET Application:** March-April
- **CUET Exam:** May-June
- **CUSB Admissions:** July-August
