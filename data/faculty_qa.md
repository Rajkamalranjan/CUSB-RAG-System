## General Faculty Information Policy

**Q: CUSB mein faculty information kahan se milegi?**

CUSB (Central University of South Bihar) mein department-specific faculty information ke liye:

### Official Channels:
1. **Department Office Direct Contact**
   - Working Hours: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:30 PM
   - Phone: 0631-2229500 (Main University Line)

2. **University Website Contact Page**
   - https://www.cusb.ac.in

3. **Email Communication**
   - Individual departments ke email addresses CUSB directory se prapt kar sakte hain

4. **Social Media/Professional Networks**
   - Many CUSB faculty members ResearchGate, LinkedIn, ya Google Scholar par available hain
   - Search: "CUSB [Department Name] faculty"

### Important Notes:
- Faculty personal contact information (mobile numbers, personal emails) privacy reasons se publicly available nahi hai
- Academic queries ke liye department offices se contact karna best option hai
- Research collaboration queries ke liye Registrar office se contact karein

- Email: dept.[name]@cusb.ac.in (standard format)
